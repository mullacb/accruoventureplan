4.2 · Marketing strategy

A naturally segmented market. Sell in order of resistance.

The B2B market naturally divides by the pension solution already in place. We’ve sequenced the market by switching friction, with small new-buyers first and large switchers last.

Segment 1 · Launch Market
Small Businesses
Sub-50 staff, already auto-enrolled into the state default. The conversation is what they’re getting back for the spend, and a visible benefit for their employees.
How we find them
Tech-forward firms sourced via the Dublin start-up community
Warm intros from accountant and payroll partners, on a commission model

Individual savers · two acquisition tracks
No PRSA yet
The mass market that incumbents don’t serve digitally. Educate via content and paid social, then convert with frictionless onboarding.
Existing PRSA holder
Already saving, but on legacy platforms with opaque fees. Prove the same value at lower cost via transparent fee comparison and one-click transfer.
No one is doing this. The digital product for the mass market hasn’t existed; PRSAs have always been sold to high-net-worth clients via brokers, so this market is a blue ocean.
