4.2 · Segmentation and channels

Sell in order of resistance. Net-new buyers first, switchers last.

Three buyer segments from the financial model, sequenced by switching friction. Concrete heuristics for finding the buyers inside each one, not category labels.

Segment 1 · volume engine
MFF Small Business
Sub-50 staff facing the My Future Fund auto-enrolment deadline. The default state scheme is the only competitor. Decisions in days.
How we find them
Tech-forward founder-led firms sourced via Dogpatch, NDRC alumni and Dublin tech-employer networks
Firms newly crossing the AE threshold, prompted by company-size triggers in CRO filings
Warm intros from accountant and payroll partners already serving the segment
