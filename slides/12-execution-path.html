4.1 · Path to launch

The first year. Getting a licence.

This is the regulatory build year, focused on acquiring the MiFID Class 3 licence. The Central Bank of Ireland targets a six-month decision from submission, but the full path including pre-application engagement, document preparation, and review is around 327 days end-to-end. Everything else, the platform build, the partner stack, the team, has to be sequenced around that clock.
