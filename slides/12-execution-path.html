4.1 · Path to launch

Twelve months from filing to first revenue. Three tracks, one launch date.

Accruo's path to first revenue is anchored to a single July 2027 launch, served by three concurrent workstreams. A regulatory track delivers MiFID Class 3 and IIA authorisation. A partner track secures the custody, fund, and insurance counterparties Accruo resells. A team and product track delivers the working platform with a closed pilot ahead of public launch. The capital ask in the previous slide funds all three.

Risk mitigation · what protects the date
01 · Tied-agent backstop

Commercial activity even if MiFID slips.

Preliminary discussions with Irish licensees to operate as a tied agent if the MiFID timeline slips beyond Q2 2027. Accruo would distribute under another firm's licence on day one, transitioning to its own authorisation when granted. Preserves go-to-market momentum without idle burn.

02 · Outsourced PCFs

Three lines of defence without overhiring.

The CBI's three-lines-of-defence requirement is met through fractional Compliance, Risk, Internal Audit, DPO and Co-Sec, not full hires. Total Y1 outsourced regulatory cost of approximately €250–450k sits inside the regulatory line of the previous slide.

03 · INED via network

No single point of failure.

The required independent non-executive director is identified through the founders' and advisor's professional network, with two named candidates already in conversation. Not contingent on a single appointment.
