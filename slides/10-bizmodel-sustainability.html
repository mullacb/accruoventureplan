3.2 · Sustainability

Three reasons our revenue is sustainable.

01 · Licensed for what's coming

The UK has eliminated broker commissions on corporate pensions; the experts we have spoken to think Ireland trails by five to ten years. By seeking a full provider licence ourselves, we are positioned for the post-broker market from day one. Regulatory licensing is a barrier to other entrants, and we happen to be a team with that skillset.

02 · We own the customer relationship

Because the PRSA is personal, the customer stays with Accruo across job changes, not with the employer or their broker. That gives us longer LTV than employer-tied schemes that reset every time someone moves job, and a direct line to the saver for cross-sell that our competitors structurally do not have.

03 · Priced structurally, not promotionally

Our 0.50% AUM fee reflects our actual cost base, not a launch discount, so it holds as the market moves toward direct distribution and lower-fee schemes. Built to be cheap, not priced to be cheap.

3.3 · Financials and the ask

Year 2 is first revenue. Y3 and Y4 are growth.

The model runs across four years. Year 1 is product build. Year 2 is first revenue. Years 3 and 4 are growth. The plan is anchored to small, addressable shares of the Irish workplace and individual pension markets.
