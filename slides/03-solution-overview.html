The Solution
2.1 · What Accruo does

A digital platform for pensions and workplace benefits. Manufactured, not brokered.

Target fee
≤50bps
Management fee on Accruo pensions, against a European workplace average of ~2%.1

HR teams get a single system to run pension, health, and life cover, replacing the broker layer between employers and providers. Individuals get a digital pension they actually control.

Accruo manufactures the pension itself, which removes the broker commission from the largest component of the benefits spend. For health and life cover, Accruo negotiates directly with carriers and brings them onto the same platform, so employers hold one relationship instead of three and employees see everything in one app.

For European workers being asked to fund their own retirement but given no real control over how, Accruo is a digital pension that lets them compare funds, see exactly what they're paying, and switch in a click, with guidance that stays with them as life changes.
