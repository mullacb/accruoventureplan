The Solution
2.1 · Solution overview

A benefits platform for HR teams. A pension app for the individual.

For HR teams, Accruo is a benefits provider. We offer a leading pension and partner with carriers for health and life cover, giving HR teams a unified experience in one app. For individuals, Accruo is a pension provider that makes saving for retirement easy.

Why two customers

Building a product HR teams love means building a product their employees love. The employee experience is what makes the HR sale stick. Once that's built, targeting individuals directly makes sense for three reasons:

01
Their own market. Sole proprietors, company directors, and people who want to save for retirement outside their workplace plan.
02
Try-before-you-buy. HR directors can sign up for Accruo themselves and use the product before deciding for their company. No Irish provider currently offers this.
03
Advocates. Employees who use Accruo at work tend to recommend it to others, and bring the product with them when they switch employers.
Why three benefits

Workplace pensions, health, and life cover are typically arranged through multiple brokers. HR carries the admin in between, sitting on top of multiple platforms. They don't want another. They want a unified experience that's plug-and-play. We provide the pension directly and partner with carriers for health and life cover, embedding their products in the same app. The result is one HR relationship, one employee app, three benefits. Companies can elect a single benefit if that's all they need.
