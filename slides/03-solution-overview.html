The Solution
2.1 · Solution overview

A benefits platform for HR teams. A pension app for the individual.

For HR teams, Accruo is a benefits provider. We offer a leading pension and partner with carriers for health and life cover, giving HR teams a unified experience in one digital platform. For individuals, Accruo is a pension provider that makes saving for retirement easy. Launching in Ireland first, we are designed to scale across Europe.

Why two customers

Building a product HR teams love means building a product their employees love. The employee experience is what makes the HR sale stick. Once that's built, targeting individuals directly makes sense for three reasons:

01
Their own market. It's a profitable market. Sole proprietors, company directors, and people who want to save for retirement outside their workplace plan. And the numbers show these clients have larger portfolios.
02
Try-before-you-buy. HR directors can sign up for Accruo themselves and use the product before deciding for their company. This is made possible by our regulatory design and isn't possible with the existing market products.
03
Advocates. Individuals using Accruo recommend it to colleagues; employees at Accruo companies carry the product into every job that follows.
Why tackle life and health cover too

Companies routinely offer pensions, health, and life cover as part of their benefits package. These are typically arranged through multiple brokers, leaving HR to manage admin across several platforms. They don't want another. They want a unified experience that's plug-and-play. We provide the pension directly and partner with carriers for health and life cover, embedding their products in the same platform. The result is one HR relationship, one employee app, three benefits. That said, companies can elect a single benefit if that's all they need.
