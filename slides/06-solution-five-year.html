2.4 · The five-year product

A platform, not a product. Three expansions deploy from one base, not rebuild.

Accruo is a platform, not a product. The launch base supports three expansions that compound: cross-border under the same EU framework, self-directed investment on the same infrastructure, and AI that climbs the value chain as regulation clarifies. Each expansion is paid for once and lands on every saver, employer, and market that follows. Deployments, not rebuilds.
