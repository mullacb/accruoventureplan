2.4 · The five-year product

Ireland is where we launch. The architecture travels.

The MVP is an Irish workplace pension with a mobile-first employee app and an HRIS-integrated employer console. The choices underneath it, a MiFID investment account rather than an insurance wrapper, an individual-account structure rather than a company scheme, and a European licence rather than a domestic one, unlock three expansions far larger than the market we win first.

Expansion 01 · Cross-border

Marie moves Dublin to Paris. Her account moves with her.

Marie joins a Dublin tech company and Accruo enrols her into a PRSA. Two years later she transfers to Paris. Her Dublin employer stops contributing, but the account stays. Her Paris employer contributes into an EuroPension in the same Accruo account.

One screen, two countries, a single projection across two tax regimes. Expansion into France, Italy, and beyond is a localisation exercise, not a relicensing one. No provider has yet brought EuroPension to France or Italy.

2.4 continued · Two more expansions from the same base

Self-direction today, on-platform advice tomorrow.

Expansion 02 · Self-directed PRSA

The first self-directed pension inside a workplace plan.

MVP: a curated fund range for a mass-market workplace pension. Later: self-direction within the same account. Default at 25, broader choice at 35, full self-direction at 45, without ever changing provider. Possible only because our MiFID wrapper gives us flexibility insurance-based incumbents do not have.

Expansion 03 · Advice in-app

AI moves up the value chain, inside the product.

The MVP AI surfaces information. As AI-in-finance regulation clarifies and our record of grounded answers builds, it moves up: personalised planning, predictive nudges, then regulated advice delivered in-app. The data advantage compounds because we see more of the employee's financial life than any trading app or single-product pension.

The compounding point: the product we ship in Ireland in 2026 is not the product we scale. It is the regulatory base from which every later product, market, and AI capability is a deployment, not a rebuild.
