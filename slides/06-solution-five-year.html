The longer-term roadmap. Cross-border, and deeper into the product.

The MVP is an Irish workplace pension with a mobile-first employee app and a payroll-platform-integrated employer console. The choices underneath it, a MiFID investment account rather than an insurance wrapper, and an individual-account structure rather than a company scheme, unlock three expansions far larger than the market we win first.

Expansion 01 · Cross-border via EuroPension

Solve cross-border pension challenges with EuroPension.

The Irish PRSA is structurally the same product as the EuroPension, sitting under the same EU regulatory framework. We can move quickly into adjacent markets, letting employees keep one account across borders and giving multinational employers a unified pension experience.

France could be the first expansion market. A localisation exercise, not a relicensing one.

Expansion 02 · Self-directed PRSA

The MVP offers a curated fund range appropriate to a mass-market workplace pension. Later, we add a self-directed PRSA: an investment account for employees whose investment knowledge and circumstances make the product suitable. This is optionality for savers who want it and can use it, not predatory expansion. Possible only because our MiFID wrapper gives us flexibility insurance-based incumbents do not have.

Expansion 03 · AI up the value chain

The MVP AI surfaces information. As AI-in-finance regulation clarifies and our record of grounded answers builds, it moves up: personalised planning, predictive nudges, then regulated advice delivered in-app.

The compounding point: the product we ship in Ireland in 2026 is not the product we scale. It is the regulatory base from which every later product, market, and AI capability is a deployment, not a rebuild.
