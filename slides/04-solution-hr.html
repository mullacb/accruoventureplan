Moment 01 · Setup

Connect once. Configure with live benchmarks.

HRIS integrations with Workday, Personio, BambooHR, HiBob. Accruo pulls employees, pushes enrolments, stays in sync. Benchmarks on every setting, contribution rate, life multiple, health excess, turn blind guessing into market comparison HR would run if the data existed.

From then on, the platform runs itself. New hires auto-enrolled; leavers offboarded; HR approves the occasional exception.

Moment 02 · AI assistant

Drafts the pensions copy, answers the pensions questions.

Trained on Irish pensions regulation, scheme documentation, and Galway Tech's configuration. Drafts benefits copy, answers routed employee questions, flags compliance nudges. HR forwards the response confident in the underlying source.
