Benefits that run themselves.

From the HR teams we spoke to, they want zero admin, benefits that attract and retain talent, and a way to benchmark those benefits against others. We provide it all.

01Integrate once.
A single connection to the HRIS already in use. Joiners and leavers flow through automatically. No double entry, no separate platform per benefit.
02Run on autopilot.
New hires are enrolled on day one without HR action. Adoption and contributions are visible live, not surfaced once a year. Approve exceptions, not the whole queue.
03Prove the spend.
Public benchmarks against the Irish tech sector mean HR can defend the package internally. The pension becomes a recruiting line, not a cost line.
