Rail between info and advice

Factual questions get grounded answers. Advice-shaped questions get redirected.

01 · Onboard

Two minutes from invite to enrolled.

WhatsApp or email invite, identity verification, a single lifecycle fund matched to retirement date. The questionnaire sits behind a button, not in the critical path.

02 · Track

The regulatory projection, as the home screen.

Monthly income projection, contribution, employer match, tax relief. An off-track projection triggers a plain-language prompt showing the adjustment and after-tax cost.

03 · Ask

Information grounded in their account.

Answers drawn from statements and Irish regulation. The rail between information and advice is hard-coded, so when a question crosses it, the assistant says so.

Unlike trading apps that treat saving like a game, or legacy pensions that treat savers like they can't be trusted with a decision, Accruo is built specifically for long-term retirement.
